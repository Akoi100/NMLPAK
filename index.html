Who We Are

Championing Excellence & Recognition

The Non-Medical Laboratory Professionals Association of Kenya (NMLPAK) is a registered professional association bringing together laboratory personnel working outside medical and clinical environments.

Our Mission

To unite, empower, and advocate for non-medical laboratory professionals through collaboration, innovation, and professional development.

Our Vision

To be the leading association championing excellence and recognition of laboratory professionals working in non clinical department in Kenya and beyond.

Where Our Members Operate

  • Mining laboratories
  • Veterinary laboratories
  • Fisheries laboratories
  • Academic institutions
  • Research institutions (e.g., KALRO)
  • School-based laboratories
  • Industrial and private laboratories
